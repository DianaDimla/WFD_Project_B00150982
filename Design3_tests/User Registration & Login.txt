1. User Registration & Login

Feature: User Registration & Login
Scenario: Customer successfully registers

- Given the customer is on the registration page
- When they enter valid details and submit
- Then their account should be created
- And they should be redirected to the homepage

Scenario: Vendor successfully registers

- Given the vendor is on the registration page
- When they provide store details and submit
- Then their account should be created
- And they should be able to list products
