3. Add to Cart & Checkout

Feature: Shopping Cart & Checkout
Scenario: Customer adds a product to the cart

- Given the customer is viewing a product
- When they click “Add to Cart”
- Then the product should appear in their cart

Scenario: Customer completes checkout

- Given the customer has items in their cart
- When they proceed to checkout and confirm payment
- Then an order should be created
- And the stock should be updated
